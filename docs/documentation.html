Getting Started

To use JawgStrap, you need to wrap your content in a block with id "jwg-strap".

<div id="jwg-strap">
HERE GOES YOUR CONTENT TO JAWGSTRAP !
</div>

Panel

Code 
<div class="container row space-between">
  <div class="panel clickable">
    <h2 class="panel-title">Panel title</h2>
    This is a clickable Panel
  </div>
  <div class="panel flex-2">
    <h2 class="panel-title">Panel title</h2>
    This is a Panel with flex-2 weight
  </div>
  <div class="panel flex-3">
    <h2 class="panel-title">Panel title</h2>
    This is a Panel with flex-3 weight
  </div>
  <div class="panel noborder">
    <h2 class="panel-title">Panel title</h2>
    This is a Panel with no border
  </div>
</div>
CSS Specifications :
  • panel-container
    • row : display panels in row direction
    • column : display panels in column direction
    • nowrap : avoid wrapping
    • centered : center the panels
    • space-between : panels take all place available in width with white space between
    • space-around : variant of space-between
    • panel
      • default : not flexible
      • flex-1 : weight of panel
      • flex-2 : weight of panel
      • flex-3 : weight of panel
      • flex-4 : weight of panel
      • flex-5 : weight of panel
      • centered : center content
      • nostyle : avoid background & box-shadow
      • noshadow : avoid box-shadow
      • clickable : add border & cursor
      • panel-title

Margin & Padding

The margin have 6 differents styles:
  • left: the margin is applied to the left of the container
  • right: the margin is applied to the right of the container
  • top: the margin is applied to the top of the container
  • bottom: the margin is applied to the bottom of the container
  • size: the margin is applied to the both sides (left & right) of the container
  • height: the margin is applied to the both vertical sides (top & bottom) of the container
And 4 differents sizes:
  • xs: 6px
  • sm: 10px
  • md: 15px
  • lg: 20px
Using the class:
[ margin ]-[ left | right | top | bottom | side | height ]-[ xs | sm | md | lg ]
